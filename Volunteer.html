Become a Paw-some Volunteer!

Every wagging tail, every purring cat, every happy adoption story at SafePaw Sanctuary is made possible by the incredible dedication of volunteers like you. Join our mission to provide love, care, and a second chance to animals in need. Your time, no matter how much, makes a world of difference!

Ways You Can Help

We have a variety of roles to fit different interests, skills, and schedules. Find where you fit in and start making a real impact:

Animal Caretaker

Assist with daily feeding, watering, cleaning of kennels/cages, and ensuring a comfortable environment for our residents.

Dog Walker / Cat Cuddler

Provide essential exercise, socialization, and affection to our dogs and cats, helping them stay happy and adoptable.

Foster Parent

Open your home temporarily to animals who need extra care or are not yet ready for adoption (e.g., puppies, sick animals).

Event Support

Help us organize and run adoption events, fundraising activities, and community outreach programs.

Adoption Counselor Assistant

Guide potential adopters through the process, answer questions, and help with paperwork to find forever homes.

Social Media / Photography

Capture heartwarming photos and videos of our animals, and help share their stories to boost adoption chances online.

Volunteer Requirements

  • Must be at least 18 years old to volunteer independently.
  • Volunteers aged 16-17 may participate with parental consent and direct adult supervision.
  • Commitment to a minimum of X hours per month (e.g., 8 hours/month) or participation in at least 2 events per quarter.
  • Ability to follow instructions, a compassionate attitude, and patience with animals.
  • All volunteers must complete our mandatory orientation and basic animal handling training.
  • Some roles may require a background check for safety.
